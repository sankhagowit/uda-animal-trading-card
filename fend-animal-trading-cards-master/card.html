Lion

Lions are the most social of all big cats

  • Scientific Name: Panthera Leo
  • Average Length: 5.6 - 8.2 ft
  • Average Lifespan: 10 - 14 years
  • Habitat: grasslands or open woodlands

Lions are big cats most commonly found in Africa. They have strong bodies weighing 330 - 550 pounds. They eat other large animals such as zebra and wildebeast. The males protect the group of lions (pride) while the females hunt for the rest of the group
